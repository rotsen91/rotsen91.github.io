Our Story

They first met while working on their undergraduate degree when they took a class together and Nestor asked Cristina if it was ok to partner up for a class project. That was the first time they talked, although years later Nestor confessed that he had his eyes on her way before that. While working together, they became good friends and remained close friends throughout the years. However, on Cristina's last semester, before finishing her undergrad, Nestor expressed the feelings he had towards her, but Crstina turned him down because she said there was no "chemistry". Boy was she wrong...

Cristina ended her last semester, and quickly moved to Boston while Nestor stayed in school. But beacuse what's meant to be will always find a way, once Nestor finished his degree, he also moved down to Boston and even started working at the same company, same department, same work projects as Cristina. He also ended up at the same apartment complex as Cristina. Life was giving her sign after sign, that maybe she should give him a chance. But she kept refusing to listen, after all, she considered him a best friend.

As time went on, they fought a lot and they laughed a lot, but most of all they enjoyed each other's company and the way they could always make each other smile. And little by little, Cristina began to fall for him without even realizing it. There's a lot more to the story but they don't want to bore you with all the details. Let's just say their story had it's roller coasters. However they always remember that the most important thing in life, is to enjoy the ride.
