IMPORTANT!!

The wedding time has changed!! The wedding will now be held on September 26, 2020 from 7 PM - 12 AM. These changes have been applied throughout the website.

Why haven't we postponed the wedding? Many guests have asked why the wedding date has not been postponed given these uncertain and difficult times. Please know that we would have postponed the wedding if we could, but we were not able to do so due to our venue and vendor signed contracts. Moving the date to 2021 means losing a substantial amount of money already invested for a future wedding date that does not guarantee a better pandemic-related outcome. Simply put, cancelling or moving the wedding date is, unfortunately, not feasible for us.

Things you should know... The wedding starts at 7 PM sharp so we strongly advice guests to arrive 30 minutes prior to the start time. Any late arrivals will have to wait in the lobby until the service ends to avoid any interruptions to the wedding ceremony. We expect our guests to dress appropriately for the occasion by wearing formal or semi-formal attire. In addition, the venue has plenty of free parking space guests can use while attending the event.

Let's talk about COVID-19... As many already know, confirmed cases of COVID-19 have been ramping up in the state of Florida during the past few months. However, we strongly encourage our guests to get informed about the latest confirmed cases in Orange County, FL and not the confirmed cases for the entire state of Florida. For the latest COVID-19 confirmed cases per US County please visit COVID-19 United States Cases by County. The wedding venue has also updated their website to include their COVID-19 safety guidelines. The venue has taken proactive measures to make the wedding party and guests feel safer by regularly sanitizing and cleaning high-touch surface areas and by taking the temperature of every guest before entering the venue. Although wearing face masks is not required by the venue, we will ask all our guests to wear face masks during the duration of the event because we want everyone to enjoy the event and have some piece of mind while doing it. Wearing masks is not the most comfortable thing but we must show respect and consideration for one another. Thank you!!

Our Story

Cristina and Nestor first met on their undergraduate degree when they took a class together and Nestor asked Cristina if she wanted to partner up for a class project. That was the first time they talked, although years later Nestor confessed to her that he had his eyes on her way before that. While working together, Cristina and Nestor became good friends and remained close friends throughout the years. However, on Cristina's last semester, before finishing her undergrad, Nestor expressed his feelings towards her, but Cristina turned him down because she said there was no "chemistry". Boy was she wrong...

Cristina ended her last semester, and quickly moved to Boston while Nestor stayed in school. But what's meant to be will always find a way. Once Nestor finished his degree, he also moved to Boston and started working at the same company as Cristina and in the same department with same work projects. He also ended up at the same apartment complex as her. Life was sending Cristina signs after signs that maybe she should give Nestor a chance. But she kept refusing to listen, after all, she considered him her best friend.

As time went on, they fought a lot and they laughed a lot, but most of all they enjoyed each other's company and the way they could always make each other smile. Little by little, Cristina began to fall for Nestor without realizing it. There's a lot more to the story but they don't want to bore you with all the details. Let's just say their story had its roller coasters. However, they always remember that the most important thing in life is to enjoy the ride with the person they love most.
